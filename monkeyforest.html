Immerse yourself in the real-life Jungle Book!

Ubud Monkey Forest is a must-see tourist destination in Bali. In this sanctuary you will find several Balinese temples, gorgeous carved stone, and hundreds of species of plants throughout more than 30 acres of lush jungle. This sacred forest is also home to over 1000 long-tailed monkeys who freely roam the park.

There are several guidelines to follow inside the sacred forest.

  • Do not run
  • Do not hide food
  • Do not disturb the babies
  • Take care of your belongings

Remember these are wild animals.

If you buy bananas to feed the monkeys, do not hide them from the monkeys. They will bite you. Monkeys are quick and will steal anything if given the chance. Make sure your purse is closed and your valuables are inside. Many people lose phones, sunglasses, and earrings.

Ubud Monkey Forest is open daily from 9 to 6.
